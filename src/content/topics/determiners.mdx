## Overview

Determiners are words that introduce nouns and express reference, quantity, or proximity.

- **Spanish**: Articles (*el, la, un, una*) agree in gender and number. Demonstratives distinguish three degrees of distance (*este, ese, aquel*). Possessives have full pronominal forms.
- **English**: Articles (*the, a, an*) do not inflect. Demonstratives have two degrees (*this, that*). Quantifiers distinguish count and mass nouns (*many, much, few, little*).
- **Chinese**: No articles. Demonstratives (*<ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby>, <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby>*) require classifiers. Numerals require classifiers. Possession is marked with *<ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>*.

## Spanish

### Articles

| | Masculine | Feminine |
|---|---|---|
| Definite singular | el | la |
| Definite plural | los | las |
| Indefinite singular | un | una |
| Indefinite plural | unos | unas |

**Examples**:
- *el libro, la mesa, los niños, las niñas*
- *un problema, una mano, unos días, unas cosas*

**Feminine with stressed a- → el**:
- *el agua, el hacha, el águila* (but: *las aguas frías*)

### Demonstratives

Three degrees of distance:

| | Masculine | Feminine | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| este | este | esta | this (near speaker) |
| ese | ese | esa | that (near listener) |
| aquel | aquel | aquella | that over there (distant) |

**Plural**:
- *estos/estas, esos/esas, aquellos/aquellas*

**Neuter** (for abstract concepts):
- *esto, eso, aquello*
- *Esto es importante.* (This is important.)

### Possessives

| | Before noun | Standalone |
|---|---|---|
| my | mi(s) | mío(s)/mía(s) |
| your | tu(s) | tuyo(s)/tuya(s) |
| his/her/its | su(s) | suyo(s)/suya(s) |
| our | nuestro(s)/nuestra(s) | nuestro(s)/nuestra(s) |
| your (pl) | vuestro(s)/vuestra(s) | vuestro(s)/vuestra(s) |
| their | su(s) | suyo(s)/suya(s) |

**Before noun**:
- *mi libro, tus amigos, su casa, nuestra familia*

**Standalone**:
- *El libro es mío.* (The book is mine.)
- *Esas son nuestras.* (Those are ours.)

### Quantifiers

| | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| todo(s)/toda(s) | all | *todos los libros* |
| cada | each/every | *cada día* |
| algunos/algunas | some | *algunos libros* |
| ningún/ninguna | no (none) | *ningún problema* |
| muchos/muchas | many | *muchos amigos* |
| pocos/pocas | few | *pocas veces* |
| varios/varias | several | *varias personas* |

**Note**: *Cada* is always singular; *ningún* shortens before masculine singular.

### Numerals

- *un libro, dos libros, tres libros, diez libros, cien libros*
- *primero* → *el primer libro* (shortened before masculine singular)

## English

### Articles

| Article | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| the | definite (specific) | the book |
| a | indefinite (singular count, consonant sound) | a book |
| an | indefinite (singular count, vowel sound) | an apple |
| zero | indefinite (plural, mass, abstract) | books, water, love |

**Note**: English articles do not agree with the noun.

### Demonstratives

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| Near | this | these |
| Far | that | those |

**Examples**:
- *this book, these books, that idea, those people*

### Possessives

| Before noun | Standalone |
|---|---|
| my | mine |
| your | yours |
| his | his |
| her | hers |
| its | (rare standalone) |
| our | ours |
| their | theirs |

**Before noun**:
- *my book, your friend, her house*

**Standalone**:
- *The book is mine.*
- *That house is ours.*

### Quantifiers

**With count nouns**:

| Quantifier | Example |
|---|---|
| many | many books |
| a few | a few friends |
| few | few problems |
| several | several people |
| both | both students |
| each/every | each book, every day |
| all (of) | all books / all of the books |

**With mass nouns**:

| Quantifier | Example |
|---|---|
| much | much water |
| a little | a little time |
| little | little money |
| some | some water |
| any | any water (negative/question) |
| no | no water |
| all (of) | all water / all of the water |

**With both**:

| Quantifier | Example |
|---|---|
| some | some books / some water |
| any | any books / any water |
| no | no books / no water |
| all | all books / all water |
| enough | enough time / enough books |
| plenty of | plenty of time / plenty of books |
| a lot of / lots of | a lot of books / a lot of water |

### Numerals

- *one book, two books, three books, ten books, a hundred books*
- *the first book, the second chapter, the third time*

## Chinese

### No articles

Chinese does not have articles:

- <ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> = book / the book / a book (context determines)
- <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>买<rt>mǎi</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> = I bought a book / I bought books / I bought the book

**Definiteness expressed through**:
- Demonstratives: <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (this book)
- Prior context
- Topic-comment structure

### Demonstratives

| | With classifier | Without (abstract) |
|---|---|---|
| this | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby> + CL | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby> |
| that | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby> + CL | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby> |
| these | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby> | — |
| those | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby> | — |

**Examples**:
- <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (this book)
- <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>个<rt>ge</rt></ruby><ruby>人<rt>rén</rt></ruby> (that person)
- <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (these books)
- <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby><ruby>人<rt>rén</rt></ruby> (those people)

### Possessives

**Possessive + <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> + Noun**:

- <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (my book)
- <ruby>你<rt>nǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>朋<rt>péng</rt></ruby><ruby>友<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby> (your friend)
- <ruby>他<rt>tā</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>家<rt>jiā</rt></ruby> (his home)

**Note**: <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> can be omitted with close relationships:
- <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>妈<rt>mā</rt></ruby> (my mom — not <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>妈<rt>mā</rt></ruby>)
- <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>爸<rt>bà</rt></ruby> (my dad)
- <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>们<rt>men</rt></ruby><ruby>学<rt>xué</rt></ruby><ruby>校<rt>xiào</rt></ruby> (our school)

### Quantifiers

| | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| <ruby>每<rt>měi</rt></ruby> | every | <ruby>每<rt>měi</rt></ruby><ruby>天<rt>tiān</rt></ruby> (every day) |
| <ruby>每<rt>měi</rt></ruby> + CL | every (counted) | <ruby>每<rt>měi</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (every book) |
| <ruby>所<rt>suǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>有<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby> | all | <ruby>所<rt>suǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>有<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (all books) |
| <ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>切<rt>qiē</rt></ruby> | all (abstract) | <ruby>一<rt>yí</rt></ruby><ruby>切<rt>qiè</rt></ruby> (everything) |
| <ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby> | some | <ruby>一<rt>yì</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby><ruby>人<rt>rén</rt></ruby> (some people) |
| <ruby>很<rt>hěn</rt></ruby><ruby>多<rt>duō</rt></ruby> | many | <ruby>很<rt>hěn</rt></ruby><ruby>多<rt>duō</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (many books) |
| <ruby>几<rt>jī</rt></ruby><ruby>个<rt>gè</rt></ruby> | a few/several | <ruby>几<rt>jǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>个<rt>ge</rt></ruby><ruby>人<rt>rén</rt></ruby> (a few people) |
| <ruby>没<rt>méi</rt></ruby><ruby>有<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby> | no | <ruby>没<rt>méi</rt></ruby><ruby>有<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (no books) |
| <ruby>别<rt>bié</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> | other | <ruby>别<rt>bié</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (other books) |

### Numerals

- <ruby>一<rt>yì</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (one book)
- <ruby>两<rt>liǎng</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (two books — <ruby>两<rt>liǎng</rt></ruby>, not <ruby>二<rt>èr</rt></ruby>)
- <ruby>三<rt>sān</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (three books)
- <ruby>十<rt>shí</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (ten books)

**Note**: <ruby>两<rt>liǎng</rt></ruby> is used with classifiers; <ruby>二<rt>èr</rt></ruby> is used in counting without classifiers.

## Comparison at a glance

| Determiner type | Spanish | English | Chinese |
|---|---|---|---|
| Definite article | el/la (gendered) | the (invariant) | None (context/demo) |
| Indefinite article | un/una (gendered) | a/an (consonant/vowel) | None (context/num) |
| Demonstrative | este/ese/aquel (3-way) | this/that (2-way) | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby>/<ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby> (2-way + CL) |
| Possessive | mi/tu/su (agrees standalone) | my/your/his (invariant) | <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>/<ruby>你<rt>nǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>/<ruby>他<rt>tā</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> (<ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>) |
| Every | cada (sing) | every/each (sing) | <ruby>每<rt>měi</rt></ruby> (+ CL) |
| All | todo(s)/toda(s) | all (pl/mass) | <ruby>所<rt>suǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>有<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby>/<ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>切<rt>qiē</rt></ruby> |
| Some | algunos/unos | some (count/mass) | <ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby> |
| No | ningún/ninguna | no | <ruby>没<rt>méi</rt></ruby><ruby>有<rt>yǒu</rt></ruby> |
| Many | muchos/muchas | many (count) | <ruby>很<rt>hěn</rt></ruby><ruby>多<rt>duō</rt></ruby> |
| Much | mucho/mucha | much (mass) | <ruby>很<rt>hěn</rt></ruby><ruby>多<rt>duō</rt></ruby> (same) |

## Common mistakes

1. **Spanish speakers learning English**: *I bought book* → *I bought **a** book* (article required)

2. **English speakers learning Spanish**: *el problema* → correct! But *la problema* → *el **problema*** (masculine)

3. **English speakers learning Chinese**: *<ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>是<rt>shì</rt></ruby><ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby>* → *<ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby>**<ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby>**<ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby>**<ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby>*** (classifier required)

4. **Chinese speakers learning English**: *I have many waters* → *I have **a lot of** water* (many vs much)
