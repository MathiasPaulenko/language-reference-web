## Overview

The preterite and imperfect distinction is one of the most challenging aspects of Spanish verb grammar. While English uses a single simple past tense, Spanish grammatically encodes a distinction between completed, bounded actions (preterite) and ongoing, habitual, or background actions (imperfect). Chinese handles past time through aspect particles like 了, 过, and 在, without verb conjugation. Choosing the wrong tense fundamentally alters the temporal framing of an event.

This distinction reflects a deeper cognitive framing: every past-tense narration involves a decision about whether the action is presented as completed or as an open-ended process. English speakers often default to the preterite because it resembles the English simple past, but this produces abrupt, chronologically disjointed narrative when the imperfect would provide background texture.

## Explanation

### Spanish

El **pretérito indefinido** expresa acciones pasadas completadas en un momento específico: *Ayer comí paella*; *Llegué, saludé y me senté*; *De pronto, decidió irse*; *Viví en Madrid durante tres años*. El **pretérito imperfecto** expresa acciones habituales, en progreso o descriptivas: *Cuando era niño, jugaba en el parque*; *El cielo era azul*; *Leía cuando sonó el teléfono*; *Tenía veinte años*. Regla mnemotécnica: pretérito = ¿qué pasó?; imperfecto = ¿cómo era? o ¿qué estaba pasando?

### English

English lacks a direct equivalent for the Spanish imperfect. The simple past covers both preterite and some imperfect uses. For habitual actions, English uses 'used to' or 'would' — 'I used to play' = *jugaba*. For ongoing interrupted actions, English uses past progressive — 'I was reading' = *leía*. Chinese aspect particles (了, 过, 在) distribute marking across the sentence rather than in verb conjugation.

### Chinese

汉语没有与西班牙语未完成时直接对应的语法形式。英语简单过去时涵盖西班牙语过去时和部分未完成时的用法。英语对习惯性动作用'used to'或'would'，对进行中被中断的动作用过去进行时。汉语通过体貌助词（了、过、在）将标记分散在句子中。过去时回答'发生了什么'，未完成时回答'是什么样的'或'当时正在发生什么'。

## Examples

| Spanish | English | Chinese |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Ayer comí paella en el restaurante. | Yesterday I ate paella at the restaurant. | 昨天我在餐厅吃了海鲜饭。 |
| Cuando era niño, jugaba en el parque. | When I was a child, I used to play in the park. | 我小时候常在公园玩。 |
| Llegué a casa y mi hermana estaba durmiendo. | I arrived home and my sister was sleeping. | 我到家时，我妹妹正在睡觉。 |
| De pronto, empezó a llover. | Suddenly, it started to rain. | 突然，开始下雨了。 |
| El cielo era azul y los pájaros cantaban. | The sky was blue and the birds were singing. | 天很蓝，鸟儿在唱歌。 |
| Viví en Madrid durante tres años. | I lived in Madrid for three years (and then left). | 我在马德里住了三年（然后离开了）。 |
| Vivía en Madrid cuando conocí a María. | I was living in Madrid when I met María. | 我（当时）住在马德里时认识了玛丽亚。 |
