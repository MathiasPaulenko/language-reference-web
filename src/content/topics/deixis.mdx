## Overview

Deixis refers to words that point to things relative to the speaker's location, time, or identity.

- **Spanish**: Has a three-way spatial distinction (near me, near you, far from both) in both demonstratives and locative adverbs. Temporal deixis includes *ahora, entonces, antes, después*.
- **English**: Two-way spatial system (this/that, here/there). Temporal deixis uses *now, then, today, yesterday, tomorrow*. Some archaic forms (*yon, yonder*) exist.
- **Chinese**: Two-way system (<ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby>/<ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby>). Locative adverbs use the same demonstrative + <ruby>里<rt>lǐ</rt></ruby>/<ruby>儿<rt>ér</rt></ruby>/<ruby>边<rt>biān</rt></ruby>. Temporal deixis is simple and context-dependent.

## Spanish

### Three-way demonstratives

| | Near speaker | Near listener | Far from both |
|---|---|---|---|
| Masculine | este | ese | aquel |
| Feminine | esta | esa | aquella |
| Neuter | esto | eso | aquello |
| Plural (masc) | estos | esos | aquellos |
| Plural (fem) | estas | esas | aquellas |

**Examples**:

- *Este libro es mío.* (This book [near me] is mine.)
- *Ese libro es tuyo.* (That book [near you] is yours.)
- *Aquel libro es de Juan.* (That book [over there] is Juan's.)

### Locative adverbs

| Near speaker | Near listener | Far from both |
|---|---|---|
| aquí | ahí | allí / allá |

- *Ven **aquí**.* (Come here [to me].)
- *Ponlo **ahí**.* (Put it there [near you].)
- *Allá en el campo...* (Way over there in the countryside...)

**Note**: *Allá* is slightly more distant/emphatic than *allí*.

### Temporal deixis

| Word | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ahora | now |
| hoy | today |
| ayer | yesterday |
| mañana | tomorrow |
| entonces | then |
| antes | before |
| después | after |
| ahora mismo | right now |
| en aquel entonces | back then |

### Personal deixis

| Person | Subject | Object | Reflexive |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | yo | me | me |
| you (informal) | tú | te | te |
| you (formal) | usted | lo/la | se |
| he/she | él/ella | lo/la | se |
| we | nosotros/nosotras | nos | nos |
| you all | vosotros/ustedes | os/los/las | os/se |
| they | ellos/ellas | los/las | se |

### Verb deixis (come/go, bring/take)

- *venir* (come toward speaker)
- *ir* (go away from speaker)
- *traer* (bring toward speaker)
- *llevar* (take away from speaker)

## English

### Two-way demonstratives

| Near speaker | Far from speaker |
|---|---|
| this | that |
| these (plural) | those (plural) |

**Examples**:

- *This book is mine.* (near me)
- *That book is yours.* (far from me, near you or distant)

### Locative adverbs

| Near speaker | Far from speaker |
|---|---|
| here | there |

- *Come **here**.* (to me)
- *Go **there**.* (away)
- *Over **there*** (more distant)
- *Right **here** / Right **there*** (emphatic)

**Archaic**: *yonder* (yon mountain = that mountain over there)

### Temporal deixis

| Word | Meaning |
|---|---|
| now | the present moment |
| then | past or future reference point |
| today | this day |
| yesterday | the day before today |
| tomorrow | the day after today |
| tonight | this evening |
| this morning | the morning of today |
| last week | the week before this one |
| next week | the week after this one |
| ago | before now (3 days ago) |
| hence | from now (3 days hence — archaic) |

### Personal deixis

| Person | Subject | Object | Reflexive |
|---|---|---|---|
| I | I | me | myself |
| you | you | you | yourself |
| he/she/it | he/she/it | him/her/it | himself/herself/itself |
| we | we | us | ourselves |
| you all | you | you | yourselves |
| they | they | them | themselves |

### Social deixis

English uses titles, honorifics, and register:

- *Mr., Mrs., Ms., Dr.*
- *sir, ma'am* (service context)
- *first names* (informal)
- *Would you mind...?* (distance-formal)

### Verb deixis (come/go, bring/take)

- *come* (toward speaker/center)
- *go* (away from speaker/center)
- *bring* (toward speaker/center)
- *take* (away from speaker/center)

## Chinese

### Two-way demonstratives

| | Near speaker | Far from speaker |
|---|---|---|
| Demonstrative | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby> zhè | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby> nà |
| Plural | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby> zhèxiē | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>些<rt>xiē</rt></ruby> nàxiē |
| Location | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>ér</rt></ruby> zhèr / <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>里<rt>lǐ</rt></ruby> zhèlǐ | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>ér</rt></ruby> nàr / <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>里<rt>lǐ</rt></ruby> nàlǐ |
| Direction | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>边<rt>biān</rt></ruby> zhèbiān | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>边<rt>biān</rt></ruby> nàbiān |
| Manner | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>样<rt>yàng</rt></ruby> zhèyàng | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>样<rt>yàng</rt></ruby> nàyàng |
| Quantity | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>么<rt>mó</rt></ruby> zhème | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>么<rt>mó</rt></ruby> nàme |
| Time | <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>时<rt>shí</rt></ruby> zhèshí | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>时<rt>shí</rt></ruby> nàshí |

**Examples**:

- <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby><ruby>是<rt>shì</rt></ruby><ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>。(This book is mine.)
- <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>本<rt>běn</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby><ruby>是<rt>shì</rt></ruby><ruby>你<rt>nǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>。(That book is yours.)

**Note**: Chinese does not distinguish "near listener" from "far from both." <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby> covers both.

### Locative adverbs

| Near speaker | Far from speaker |
|---|---|
| <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>er</rt></ruby> / <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>里<rt>lǐ</rt></ruby> | <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>er</rt></ruby> / <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>里<rt>lǐ</rt></ruby> |

- <ruby>到<rt>dào</rt></ruby><ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>er</rt></ruby><ruby>来<rt>lái</rt></ruby>。(Come here.)
- <ruby>去<rt>qù</rt></ruby><ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>er</rt></ruby>。(Go there.)

**Directional**:

- <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>边<rt>biān</rt></ruby> (this side)
- <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>边<rt>biān</rt></ruby> (that side)

### Temporal deixis

| Word | Meaning |
|---|---|
| <ruby>现<rt>xiàn</rt></ruby><ruby>在<rt>zài</rt></ruby> | now |
| <ruby>今<rt>jīn</rt></ruby><ruby>天<rt>tiān</rt></ruby> | today |
| <ruby>昨<rt>zuó</rt></ruby><ruby>天<rt>tiān</rt></ruby> | yesterday |
| <ruby>明<rt>míng</rt></ruby><ruby>天<rt>tiān</rt></ruby> | tomorrow |
| <ruby>刚<rt>gāng</rt></ruby><ruby>才<rt>cái</rt></ruby> | just now |
| <ruby>以<rt>yǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>前<rt>qián</rt></ruby> | before |
| <ruby>以<rt>yǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>后<rt>hòu</rt></ruby> | after |
| <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>时<rt>shí</rt></ruby> | then |
| <ruby>当<rt>dāng</rt></ruby><ruby>时<rt>shí</rt></ruby> | at that time |
| <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>时<rt>shí</rt></ruby> | at this time |

### Personal deixis

| Person | Pronoun |
|---|---|
| I | <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby> |
| you | <ruby>你<rt>nǐ</rt></ruby> |
| you (formal) | <ruby>您<rt>nín</rt></ruby> |
| he/she/it | <ruby>他<rt>tā</rt></ruby> / <ruby>她<rt>tā</rt></ruby> / <ruby>它<rt>tā</rt></ruby> |
| we | <ruby>我<rt>wǒ</rt></ruby><ruby>们<rt>men</rt></ruby> |
| you all | <ruby>你<rt>nǐ</rt></ruby><ruby>们<rt>men</rt></ruby> |
| they | <ruby>他<rt>tā</rt></ruby><ruby>们<rt>men</rt></ruby> / <ruby>她<rt>tā</rt></ruby><ruby>们<rt>men</rt></ruby> |

**Social deixis**: <ruby>您<rt>nín</rt></ruby> (formal you) vs <ruby>你<rt>nǐ</rt></ruby> (informal)

### <ruby>来<rt>lái</rt></ruby>/<ruby>去<rt>qù</rt></ruby> as verb deixis

- <ruby>来<rt>lái</rt></ruby> (come toward speaker)
- <ruby>去<rt>qù</rt></ruby> (go away from speaker)
- <ruby>带<rt>dài</rt></ruby><ruby>来<rt>lái</rt></ruby> (bring toward speaker)
- <ruby>带<rt>dài</rt></ruby><ruby>去<rt>qù</rt></ruby> (take away from speaker)
- <ruby>过<rt>guò</rt></ruby><ruby>来<rt>lái</rt></ruby> (come over here)
- <ruby>过<rt>guò</rt></ruby><ruby>去<rt>qù</rt></ruby> (go over there)

## Examples in context

### Come here
- **ES**: *Ven **aquí**.* (to me)
- **EN**: *Come **here**.*
- **ZH**: <ruby>到<rt>dào</rt></ruby><ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>er</rt></ruby><ruby>来<rt>lái</rt></ruby>。

### Put it there (near you)
- **ES**: *Ponlo **ahí**.*
- **EN**: *Put it **there**.*
- **ZH**: <ruby>放<rt>fàng</rt></ruby><ruby>在<rt>zài</rt></ruby><ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>er</rt></ruby>。

### That man over there
- **ES**: *Aquel hombre* (far from both)
- **EN**: *That man over there.*
- **ZH**: <ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>边<rt>biān</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>个<rt>ge</rt></ruby><ruby>人<rt>rén</rt></ruby>。

## Common mistakes

1. **English speakers learning Spanish**: Using ese for far objects: *Ese montaña* → *Aquel montaña* (if far from both)

2. **Spanish speakers learning English**: Over-specifying distance: *That there* → *That* or *That over there*

3. **Spanish speakers learning Chinese**: Expecting three-way distinction: no aquí/ahí/allí in Chinese, just <ruby>这<rt>zhè</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>ér</rt></ruby>/<ruby>那<rt>nà</rt></ruby><ruby>儿<rt>ér</rt></ruby>

4. **Chinese speakers learning Spanish**: Using ese for all "that": must distinguish ese (near you) from aquel (far)
