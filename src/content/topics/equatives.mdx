## Overview

Equatives express that two entities are equal in some quality.

- **Spanish**: Uses *tan...como* for adjectives/adverbs, *tanto...como* for nouns/verbs, *igual de...que* as an alternative.
- **English**: Uses *as...as* for all categories. *The same as* for identity. *Twice/three times as...as* for multiples.
- **Chinese**: Uses *<ruby>和<rt>hé</rt></ruby>/<ruby>跟<rt>gēn</rt></ruby>...<ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>样<rt>yàng</rt></ruby>* for equality, *<ruby>不<rt>bù</rt></ruby><ruby>如<rt>rú</rt></ruby>* for negative equality. Multiples use *<ruby>倍<rt>bèi</rt></ruby>*.

## Spanish

### Tan...como (adjectives/adverbs)

- *Juan es **tan alto como** María.* (John is as tall as Mary.)
- *Corre **tan rápido como** yo.* (He runs as fast as me.)
- *No es **tan difícil como** parece.* (It's not as difficult as it seems.)

### Tanto...como (nouns/verbs)

- *Tiene **tanto dinero como** yo.* (He has as much money as me.)
- *Lee **tanto como** ella.* (He reads as much as she does.)
- *No tengo **tantos libros como** tú.* (I don't have as many books as you.)

**Note**: *Tanto* agrees in gender and number:
- *tanto* (masc sing) / *tanta* (fem sing) / *tantos* (masc pl) / *tantas* (fem pl)

### Igual de...que / igual que

- *Es **igual de alto que** María.* (He's as tall as Mary.)
- *Corre **igual de rápido que** yo.* (He runs as fast as me.)
- *Es **igual que** su padre.* (He's the same as his father.)

### Negative

- *No es **tan alto como** María.* (He's not as tall as Mary.)
- *No tiene **tanto dinero como** yo.* (He doesn't have as much money as me.)

### Multiples

- *Es **dos veces más alto** que María.* (He's twice as tall as Mary.)
- *Es **el doble de alto**.* (He's twice as tall.)
- *Tiene **el triple de** dinero.* (He has three times as much money.)

## English

### As...as

- *John is **as tall as** Mary.*
- *He runs **as fast as** I do.*
- *It's not **as difficult as** it seems.*

**Note**: Use subject pronoun + auxiliary in formal English:
- *as fast as **I do*** / *as fast as **me*** (informal)

### The same as

- *This is **the same as** that.*
- *He looks **the same as** his brother.*

### Just as...as

- *It's **just as** important **as** the other one.*
- *She's **just as** smart **as** her sister.*

### Not so...as / not as...as

- *He's **not as tall as** Mary.*
- *He's **not so tall as** Mary.* (more formal/archaic)

### Almost / nearly as...as

- *He's **almost as tall as** Mary.*
- *It's **nearly as difficult as** the other one.*

### Half / twice / three times as...as

- *He's **half as tall as** his father.*
- *He's **twice as tall as** his brother.*
- *It's **three times as expensive**.*

### As + noun + as

- *I have **as much money as** you.*
- *She has **as many books as** I do.*
- *I drank **as little water as** possible.*

## Common mistakes

1. **English speakers learning Spanish**: *Juan es alto como María* → *Juan es **tan** alto **como** María*

2. **Spanish speakers learning English**: *John is so tall as Mary* → *John is **as** tall **as** Mary*

3. **English speakers learning Chinese**: *<ruby>约<rt>yuē</rt></ruby><ruby>翰<rt>hàn</rt></ruby><ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>样<rt>yàng</rt></ruby><ruby>高<rt>gāo</rt></ruby><ruby>玛<rt>mǎ</rt></ruby><ruby>丽<rt>lì</rt></ruby>* → *<ruby>约<rt>yuē</rt></ruby><ruby>翰<rt>hàn</rt></ruby>**<ruby>和<rt>hé</rt></ruby>**<ruby>玛<rt>mǎ</rt></ruby><ruby>丽<rt>lì</rt></ruby>**<ruby>一<rt>yī</rt></ruby><ruby>样<rt>yàng</rt></ruby><ruby>高<rt>gāo</rt></ruby>***

4. **Chinese speakers learning English**: *John as tall as Mary* → *John **is** as tall as Mary* (copula required)
