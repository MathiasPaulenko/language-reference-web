## Overview

When multiple adjectives modify a noun, their order is governed by rules that vary across languages.

- **Spanish**: Flexible order with preferences. Evaluative adjectives (hermoso, terrible) usually precede descriptive ones (grande, viejo). Some adjectives change meaning by position (*un hombre grande* = a big man; *un gran hombre* = a great man).
- **English**: Rigid order following the mnemonic OSASCOMP (opinion, size, age, shape, color, origin, material, purpose). Violations sound distinctly foreign.
- **Chinese**: All modifiers precede the noun. The order is generally: evaluative > size > color > shape > material. <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> links adjectives to nouns.

## Spanish

### Default order

**Evaluative before descriptive**:

- *un **hermoso** coche **rojo*** (a beautiful red car)
- *una **interesante** película **larga*** (an interesting long film)

**Descriptive before evaluative** is also possible, with a slight shift in emphasis:

- *un coche rojo hermoso* (emphasis on the car being red and beautiful)

### Meaning-changing position

Some Spanish adjectives change meaning based on position:

| Before noun | After noun |
|---|---|
| *un gran hombre* (a great man) | *un hombre grande* (a big man) |
| *mi antiguo jefe* (my former boss) | *mi jefe antiguo* (my old boss [in age]) |
| *una pobre mujer* (a poor woman [pitiful]) | *una mujer pobre* (a poor woman [no money]) |
| *cierta persona* (a certain person) | *una persona cierta* (a trustworthy person) |
| *diferentes opiniones* (various opinions) | *opiniones diferentes* (differing opinions) |

### Coordinate adjectives

With *y/e*:

- *una película **larga y aburrida*** (a long and boring film)
- *un coche **rápido e inteligente*** (a fast and smart car)

### Multiple adjectives without coordination

- *una casa **blanca grande**.* (a big white house.)
- *un libro **interesante antiguo español**.* (an interesting old Spanish book.)

**Note**: This order is less fixed than English.

## English

### OSASCOMP order

Mnemonic for adjective order:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **O**pinion | beautiful, ugly, lovely, terrible |
| **S**ize | big, small, huge, tiny |
| **A**ge | old, young, ancient, new |
| **S**hape | round, square, flat, oval |
| **C**olor | red, blue, green, black |
| **O**rigin | Italian, Chinese, American |
| **M**aterial | wooden, metal, silk, plastic |
| **P**urpose | racing, sleeping, hunting, walking |

**Examples**:

- a **beautiful old Italian car**
- a **big round red wooden table**
- a **lovely little old rectangular green French iron writing desk**
- a **terrible huge new oval blue American plastic swimming pool**

**Note**: Native speakers rarely use more than 3-4 adjectives.

### Coordinate adjectives

When adjectives are from the same category, they are "coordinate" and separated by commas or *and*:

- a **long, boring** film (opinion + opinion)
- a **big and ugly** building (opinion + opinion)
- a **red and blue** flag (color + color)

**Test**: If you can insert "and" between them and reverse the order, they are coordinate:

- ✅ a **boring and long** film / a **long and boring** film → coordinate
- ❌ a **red big** ball → not coordinate (must be "big red")

### Postpositive adjectives (rare)

A few fixed expressions have postpositive adjectives:

- *attorney **general***
- *court **martial***
- *heir **apparent***
- *notary **public***
- *something **interesting*** (when modified by indefinite pronouns)
- *the president **elect***

## Chinese

### All modifiers before noun

Chinese never places adjectives after the noun:

- <ruby>好<rt>hǎo</rt></ruby><ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby> (good book)
- <ruby>漂<rt>piào</rt></ruby><ruby>亮<rt>liang</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>车<rt>chē</rt></ruby> (beautiful car)

### Default order

| Order | Category | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Evaluation / modality | <ruby>好<rt>hǎo</rt></ruby>, <ruby>漂<rt>piāo</rt></ruby><ruby>亮<rt>liàng</rt></ruby>, <ruby>重<rt>zhòng</rt></ruby><ruby>要<rt>yào</rt></ruby> |
| 2 | Size / quantity | <ruby>大<rt>dà</rt></ruby>, <ruby>小<rt>xiǎo</rt></ruby>, <ruby>多<rt>duō</rt></ruby> |
| 3 | Color | <ruby>红<rt>hóng</rt></ruby>, <ruby>蓝<rt>lán</rt></ruby>, <ruby>绿<rt>lǜ</rt></ruby> |
| 4 | Shape | <ruby>圆<rt>yuán</rt></ruby>, <ruby>方<rt>fāng</rt></ruby> |
| 5 | Material | <ruby>木<rt>mù</rt></ruby>, <ruby>铁<rt>tiě</rt></ruby>, <ruby>塑<rt>sù</rt></ruby><ruby>料<rt>liào</rt></ruby> |
| 6 | Noun | <ruby>车<rt>chē</rt></ruby>, <ruby>书<rt>shū</rt></ruby>, <ruby>桌<rt>zhuō</rt></ruby><ruby>子<rt>zǐ</rt></ruby> |

- <ruby>一<rt>yì</rt></ruby><ruby>辆<rt>liàng</rt></ruby><ruby>漂<rt>piào</rt></ruby><ruby>亮<rt>liang</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>大<rt>dà</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>红<rt>hóng</rt></ruby><ruby>色<rt>sè</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>木<rt>mù</rt></ruby><ruby>头<rt>tóu</rt></ruby><ruby>车<rt>chē</rt></ruby> (a beautiful big red wooden car)

**Note**: Multiple <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> is grammatical but can be heavy. In natural speech, some <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> are often dropped:

- <ruby>一<rt>yì</rt></ruby><ruby>辆<rt>liàng</rt></ruby><ruby>漂<rt>piào</rt></ruby><ruby>亮<rt>liang</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>大<rt>dà</rt></ruby><ruby>红<rt>hóng</rt></ruby><ruby>木<rt>mù</rt></ruby><ruby>车<rt>chē</rt></ruby> (a beautiful big red wooden car — one <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>)

### <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> omission

Monosyllabic adjectives in fixed expressions often drop <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby>:

- <ruby>红<rt>hóng</rt></ruby><ruby>花<rt>huā</rt></ruby> (red flower)
- <ruby>白<rt>bái</rt></ruby><ruby>马<rt>mǎ</rt></ruby> (white horse)
- <ruby>大<rt>dà</rt></ruby><ruby>街<rt>jiē</rt></ruby> (big street)

But with modified nouns, <ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby> is preferred:

- <ruby>红<rt>hóng</rt></ruby><ruby>色<rt>sè</rt></ruby><ruby>的<rt>de</rt></ruby><ruby>花<rt>huā</rt></ruby> (red-colored flower)

### Compound adjectives

Chinese sometimes compounds adjectives before the noun:

- <ruby>高<rt>gāo</rt></ruby><ruby>大<rt>dà</rt></ruby> (tall and big)
- <ruby>干<rt>gān</rt></ruby><ruby>净<rt>jìng</rt></ruby> (clean)
- <ruby>整<rt>zhěng</rt></ruby><ruby>齐<rt>qí</rt></ruby> (tidy)
